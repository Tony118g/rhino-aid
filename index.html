About Us

Rhino Aid is dedicated to conserving the rhino population in Africa. We do this by raising awareness and providing ways in which anyone from anywhere can help. It is crucial that we act now as the population is steadily decreasing and unless we take action, we may witness the loss of yet another incredible species entirely.

The problem

The number one problem that rhino's in Africa have faced to date is poaching. Their keratin horns are highly sort after by poachers for illegal trade and thus more and more rhino are killed each year. With populations dwindling and the demand for horns staying at an all time high, the fate of these magnificent creatures is seemingly inevitable unless we do something about it.
