About Us

Rhino Aid is dedicated to conserving the rhino population in Africa. We do this by raising awareness and providing ways in which anyone from anywhere can help. It is crucial that we act now as the population is steadily decreasing and unless we take action, we may witness the loss of yet another incredible species entirely.

The problem

The number one problem that rhino's in Africa have faced to date is poaching. Their keratin horns are highly sort after by poachers for illegal trade and thus more and more rhino are killed each year. With populations dwindling and the demand for horns staying at an all time high, the fate of these magnificent creatures is seemingly inevitable unless we do something about it. How you can help!

Although the situation seems dire it's not too late! Below are ways you can help no matter who or where you are.

1-Volunteer

Volunteering is one of the best ways to get hands on and really make a difference. Head over to our volunteer page and check out the projects that you could join to help the rhino first hand!

2-Donate

No matter where you are you can take part in the rhino's fight against extinction. Head over to our donate page to see where you can offer much needed financial support to organisations involved in rhino protection.

3-Spread the word

Tell your friends about the rhino situation in Africa! The more people that know about the situation, the bigger the cause grows. So spread the word and let the world know that it needs to fight for its beloved creatures.
