About Us

Rhino Aid is dedicated to conserving the rhino population in Africa. We do this by raising awareness and providing ways in which anyone from anywhere can help. It is crucial that we act now as the population is steadily decreasing and unless we take action, we may witness the loss of yet another incredible species entirely.
