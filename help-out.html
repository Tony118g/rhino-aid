Volunteer!

Have a look below for volunteer projects that you could join!

Care For Wild-Rhino Sanctuary

This is the largest orphan rhino sanctuary in the world and is host to some pretty spectacular and outstanding conservation work! When it comes to making a difference these guys are the top of the class. The best part is that you could be part of it! If this sounds like something that would interest you then take a look at their website for the full details and see how you could volunteer with the!.

Go to Care For Wild website

Imire

Imire is a truly wonderful place and is definitely a worth while venture when it comes to volunteering there! On top of the rhino, they offer various other volunteer projects on their program so feel free to explore their page for more info.

Go to Imire Website.

Save The Rhino

If you're interested in a more behind the scenes volunteering approach then this is the ideal site for you to visit. "Save The Rhino" have volunteer opportunites in less frontline areas such as office, events and corporate volunteering. This way you can still take part hands on without having to go to the actual rhinos themselves and can work in more of a supportive role.

Go to Save The Rhino website
