Help Out!

Have a look below for a few suggestions on where you can volunteer or donate in an effort towards rhino conservation.

Care For Wild-Rhino Sanctuary

This is the largest orphan rhino sanctuary in the world and is host to some pretty spectacular and outstanding conservation work! When it comes to making a difference these guys are the top of the class. The best part is that you could be part of it! If this sounds like something that would interest you then take a look at their volunteer or donate pages to see how you can join their fight!

Volunteer Page Donate Page

Imire

Imire is a truly wonderful place that provides refuge to various kinds of wildlife including rhino. If you are looking for a broad ranging volunteer experience or just want to donate to a worthy cause then this is for you!

Volunteer Page Donate Page

Save The Rhino

If you are interested in a more supportive approach to volunteering or want to donate somewhere but dont know which specific organisation to donate to then this is for you. Save the rhino offers a range of more behind the scenes volunteering such as office, corporate or event volunteering where you can take part in creating a more broad impact in rhino conservation. Donating to them means you dont have to choose a particular place yourself, instead you can donate to their more than worthy cause!
